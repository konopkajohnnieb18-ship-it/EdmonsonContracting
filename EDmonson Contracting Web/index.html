Fence & Deck Guide for Molino & Pensacola Homeowners

What to know before you build: materials, permits, pricing ranges, and how Edmonson Contracting delivers clean, durable results in Northwest Florida.

1) Choosing the Right Fence

In Escambia County, privacy fences are the most requested because they block wind, add security, and help tame the Florida sun. Pressure-treated pine is affordable and strong; cedar resists insects and brings a warmer look; composites reduce maintenance. We set posts deeper than bare minimum, use quality concrete, and align rails true so your fence line stays straight. Gates are the stress point—upgraded hinges and latches prevent sagging and make daily use smoother.

2) Decks That Feel Solid Underfoot

The difference between a “nice deck” and a great deck is what you can’t see: footings, fasteners, and framing. We build on a level foundation, use corrosion-resistant hardware for coastal air, and finish with clean board spacing and flush edges. Wood is timeless; modern composites handle moisture and heavy traffic. Add rail lighting or steps for safer evenings outside.

3) Pergolas, Patios & Shade

A pergola adds structure and shade without closing off your yard. We match roof slat angles to the sun path, and we can integrate metal accents or privacy screens. Patios—concrete or pavers—benefit from proper grading and drainage so you aren’t battling puddles after summer showers.

4) Building Near the Coast: Docks & Hardware

Salt air and sun are hard on materials. For docks and waterfront builds, we spec tougher hardware, UV-resistant finishes, and smarter fasteners. The result: a structure that holds up to tide, time, and regular use.

5) Permits, HOA & Timeline

Some projects require permits or HOA approval. We explain what’s needed up front and help navigate the paperwork so there are no surprises. A typical fence install is 1–3 days once materials arrive; decks and pergolas vary by size and site conditions. We set clear expectations on day one and update you as we work.

6) Budget Ranges (Every Yard is Different)

Pricing depends on footage, terrain, and material selection. During your free quote we measure, discuss options, and give a transparent breakdown. Many homeowners start with a privacy fence and later add a deck, pergola, or patio in phases—planning ahead keeps the whole space cohesive.
